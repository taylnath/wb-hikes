Proxy Falls Hike

Photo taken by me (Nathan Taylor).

Normally this is a busy trail, but since the road is currently closed there were only a few people out on the trail. Some highlights of the hike:
  • Hiking on a closed highway with some nice views.
  • Climbing over snow-covered lava beds at the top.
  • The actual trail to the waterfall was fairly short and easy once we got to the top.

Three-mile Lake Dunes Hike

Photo taken by me (Nathan Taylor).

This was supposed to be an easy 6-mile hike, but it turned into a 10+-mile hike since we parked at the wrong trailhead. It was still fun! Some highlights of the hike:
  • A variety of landscape and scenery: sandy dunes, forest, hills, and a lake.
  • Newts! There were a bunch of newts in the forested areas.
  • The beach was relatively warm (usually when I go it's pretty windy).
