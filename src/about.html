How it started

In 2015 I acquired a Nalgene brand water bottle that said "I heart rec" on the side. I used this bottle occasionally for the past 6 years or so.

This past year, I was using it as my work water container. One day, I accidentally left it at work, on a counter right at the front of the store. When I returned a few days later, the bottle was missing! I asked a few people about it, and they recalled seeing the bottle in the same place I left it, just one day before I returned to work. It turned out someone had thrown it in the trash. At least I knew there was no hope of getting it back (the trash had already gone into the compactor).

In the meantime, my boss had already ordered a new water bottle of the same type. As a way to say thanks, I decided to take the water bottle with me on hikes, and take pictures of places it had been. This website is a compilation of those pictures and some stories. Thanks, Elysse!
